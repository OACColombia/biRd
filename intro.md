## 1. What is biRd?

- **An R package to integrate different bird datasets** — birds in R
- Unify taxonomy
- Combine phylogenetic information
- Assess functional traits available at the species level
- Report IUCN category

> **Important clarification:** This package is based on available datasets.

---

## 2. Datasets

- AviList: The Global Avian Checklist
- A complete and dynamic tree of birds
- AVONET: morphological, ecological and geographical data for all birds
